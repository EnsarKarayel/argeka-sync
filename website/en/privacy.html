Privacy Policy

ARGEKA Sync runs self-hosted. Transfer data, connection settings and user records stay in the database on the computer or server where the product is installed.

Website data

The website does not collect account registrations or product data. Standard hosting access logs may be processed by the hosting provider.

Product data

Source and target database details stay in your own environment. ARGEKA Sync does not send this data to a central SaaS system.

Support

Information you share for support is used only to handle the support request.
